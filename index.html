Startup Services

A Start up is a venture to explore business ideas focusing any typical business solution, skill or model.

There are lakhs of start-ups in India who are promoted by young entrepreneurs to enter into the business world. The start-up venture is founded and based upon any new technological solutions, social cause, I T products, engineering field etc.

The Indian Government has promoted Start up India project and also launched various schemes for the start-ups.

Our team provides various services to the start-ups. These services include company registration, funding & finance, mentoring, CFO Services, project consultancy, I P R relate services, Accounts & Audits, taxation, outsourcing, valuation, due diligence, etc.

Our team shoulders the responsibility of complete outsourcing of compliances under various acts, regulations, banking, accounts etc. so that the entrepreneurs can concentrate on their own business rather than nitty-gritty of the compliances.

Our team provides tailor made solutions to the start-ups. We understand the eco-system of business venture. Our professional team believes that we grow with each other and empower the start-ups to enter into the business with confidence and achieve their targets in the desired manner.
